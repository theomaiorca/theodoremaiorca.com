4-bit Digital-to-Analog Converter (DAC)

Designed and simulated a 4-bit DAC using summing op-amps and an R-2R resistor ladder in LTSpice, achieving 16 discrete voltage steps. Fabricated a 1" × 1" PCB in Autodesk Fusion 360 using SMD components and verified analog output with a binary counter and oscilloscope.
