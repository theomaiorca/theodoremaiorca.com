Arduino Light vs Sound Reaction Time Tester

Wanted to actually know whether you react faster to sound or light. Built a setup to test it: Arduino in C++, hardware interrupts for millisecond timing, randomized light and sound stimuli, results live on an LCD. Turns out sound is faster, usually.

3-Person Lab Project · Cal Poly SLO

4-bit Digital-to-Analog Converter (DAC)

First time I went from a schematic all the way to a physical PCB. Simulated a 4-bit DAC in LTSpice using an R-2R resistor ladder — two resistor values, 16 voltage levels. Laid it out on a 1"×1" board in Fusion 360, soldered the SMD parts, verified output on an oscilloscope. All 16 steps came out clean.
